Overview

PatchSignal helps prioritize exploited vulnerabilities by combining source data with CVSS severity, EPSS likelihood, remediation actions, due dates, and exploitation context.

The goal is to make vulnerability triage easier by surfacing the records most relevant to active exploitation, severity, and response planning.

PatchSignal is an independent enrichment project intended for research, learning, and personal security workflow support. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by CISA, NIST, FIRST, or any source provider.

What PatchSignal Shows

  • CVE identifier
  • Affected vendor and product
  • CVSS score and severity
  • EPSS score and percentile
  • Date added
  • Remediation due date
  • Required action or remediation context
  • Ransomware-use indicator when available
  • Description and supporting context
